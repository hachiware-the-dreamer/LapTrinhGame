# 🎮 Whack-a-Zombie

Whack-a-Zombie is an interactive 2D game where players must click on zombie heads that appear at multiple distinct spawn locations across the screen. The game tracks performance through a HUD displaying hits, misses, and a calculated accuracy percentage. To succeed, players must react quickly as zombies may automatically disappear after a short duration. Happy gaming!

---

# 🕹️ How to Play
* **Objective:** Click on zombie heads that appear at multiple spawn locations across the playfield.
* **Timer:** Be quick! Each zombie only persists for a short duration (800-1500 ms) before vanishing.
* **Controls:** Use your **Mouse Left-Click** to hit the targets.
* **Scoring:** The HUD tracks your Hits, Misses, and overall Accuracy percentage.
